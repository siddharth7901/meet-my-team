creative director

Creative Directors manage the art and graphics of promotional products in the fields of advertising and business. Experience included on sample resumes of Creative Directors includes rebranding a company's color scheme, logo, and brand elements; and designing an app user's interface via software like Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. The job of a Creative Director is a stressful one, so employers look for several years of experience on a candidate's resume, as well as an undergraduate degree in the fields of either marketing or graphic design.

project manager

A project manager is a professional in the field of project management. Project managers have the responsibility of the planning, procurement and execution of a project, in any undertaking that has a defined scope, defined start and a defined finish; regardless of industry.
